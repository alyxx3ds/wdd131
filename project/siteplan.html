Happy Paws Pet Adoption Center

Site Name

Happy Paws Pet Adoption Center

A fictional nonprofit dedicated to rescuing and rehoming dogs, cats, and small animals.

Optional domain: happypaws-adopt.org (simulated availability)

Site Purpose

The website will:

  • Showcase adoptable pets with searchable/filterable profiles.
  • Educate visitors on the adoption process and requirements.
  • Provide tools to submit adoption applications or volunteer inquiries.
  • Share success stories to encourage adoptions and donations.

Scenarios (Target Audience Questions)

  1. "How do I know if a pet is a good fit for my apartment?"
  2. "What’s the fee to adopt a cat, and what does it include?"
  3. "Can I volunteer without adopting an animal?"
